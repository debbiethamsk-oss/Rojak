# TITLE : The Active Ager 

## Summary
Encourage or motivate older people to be more outdoors, physically active and sociable.
As we grow older, we become less active, mobile or disinterested in being so.
This project focuses on how to encourage , movtivate or inspire the reverse.
Additionally for individuals who love being alone, these activities can spur ones attitude, 
self-intiated onn preferred activities so as to feel inclusive, fulfilled  and not lonesome, alineated or isolated.

## Challenges
Initiating a project like this is a huge endeavor.  Dealing with people , in this case ageing people takes sepcialzed approaches.
We will need  :
* funding to run such project, projects
* carefully navigation of legal,ethical requirements include informed consent, , data privacy, compliance with accessibility regulations
* Liability and insurance protection. Volunteer managments
* Safety and environmental requirements - eg accessibility, Site selection , emergency protocols, weather and environmental protection, etc
* Staffing and Supervision - managing health (eg dementia) conditions, specially trained in geriatic physical and safety activities and precautions 
* Public Space Usage - compliance with local regulations 
* additionally follow through and follow-up personnel or individuals to ensure participarticipants are constantly cared for, monitored, etc
  
Challenges to solve / implement
* it is a huge undertaking after delving into the nitty gritties.  the initial goal of just inspiring or motivating seniors to be more outdoors
  or more active seemed like a simple goal but the legal structure, safety certifications and specific accessibility standards are immense.
  To start such a project is also capital intensive that required regulatory transparency and reporting. Risk management is critical with an aging population
  who are more prone to falls or medical emergencise.   The need for mandatory professional indemnity insurance need to be fulfilled.
